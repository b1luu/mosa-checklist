Closing Checklist

Complete every task before locking up.

General / Front Tasks

  • Bring in menu stands
  • Wipe down all tables and chairs
  • Wipe down trash cans with Bleach Solution if visibly dirty

Equipment Cleaning

Wash all equipment, including:

  • Spoons and Tongs
  • Shakers
  • Tea Pots
  • Syrup shot cups
  • Measuring cups
  • Sliding lid and draining rack of the boba station
  • Juice Blender and lid

Inventory Check (AT LEAST)

  • 1 dispenser of Genmai (2 for Fri/Sat)
  • 1 dispenser of Buckwheat Barley (2 for Fri/Sat)
  • 2 batches of Tea Jelly (3 for Fri/Sat)
  • 2 batches of Hun Kue (3 for Fri/Sat)
  • 2 bottles of Matcha Concentrate
  • 2 boxes of Pistachio Paste
  • 1 box of Roasted Pistachio

Devices and POS

  • Charge tablets if needed (DoorDash and HP) and put back
  • Turn off music and charge phone if needed
  • On POS, check everything back in stock

Ingredient Storage

  • Move all food ingredients to fridge

Move teas to fridge:

  • Buckwheat Barley Tea
  • Genmai Tea
  • TGY Oolong -> move into drink pitcher (use for tea jelly)
  • Black Tea -> store in small containers and move into freezer

Kitchen Cleaning

  • Clean the sealer, turn it off, cut off used portion

Clean sink strainers:

  • 3-compartment sink
  • Boba washing sink
  • Disassemble fruit slicer and wash it

Empty draining trays:

  • 2 under kitchen sink
  • 1 under boba station
  • Clean leftover drainage trash in sewage sink (storage area)

Mop / Vacuum Machine

  • Empty and wash dirty tank
  • Put into auto-clean mode
  • Refill clean tank (10ml cleaning solution + water)

Restroom and Supplies

  • Refill hand towel paper and hand soaps if below 1/2
  • Refill toilet paper / seat paper if needed
  • Clean restroom sink and toilet with bleach solution
  • Sanitize towels with bleach

Floor Cleaning (Front)

  • Sweep and mop bar area
  • Sweep and mop restroom floor

Floor Cleaning (Back)

  • Sweep and mop kitchen area
  • Sweep and mop storage area

Trash and Disposal

  • Throw all trash (including breaking down empty boxes)
  • Take out all five trash bins and re-bag

Throw out if any left:

  • Boba
  • Cream Foam (Pistachio, Brown Sugar, Chestnut)
  • Green Tea
  • 4 Season Tea

Final Cleaning Tasks

  • Clean boba washing sink strain (hot water + Simple Green)
  • Take all personal items from fridge/freezer/break area
  • Check Costco supplies and report if running low (list on freezer)
  • Use bleach spray and towel to wipe all surfaces

Machine Shutdown

Turn off machines:

  • Sugar Dispenser (refill fully after turning off)
  • Boba Warmer (unplug)
  • Prep Station (unplug)

Store Closing Security

  • Turn off all lights, fan, and AC
  • Lock storage gate
  • Arm security system (arm away)
  • Lock front door
